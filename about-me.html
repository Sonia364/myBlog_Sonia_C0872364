About Sonia Nain

Hi, I'm Sonia! I am a web developer based in Toronto, Canada. I have a passion for creating websites and mobile applications that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional.

I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Application degree from MCM DAV College in Chandigarh, India in 2017. I am currently pursuing a Mobile Application Design and Development program at Lambton College in Toronto, Canada.

I started my professional career as a Jr. Web Developer at Deftsoft, where I worked from 2018 to 2021. During my time there, I gained experience in developing and maintaining websites for various clients. In 2021, I joined Fresco as a Sr. Web Developer, where I am currently working on building web applications using the latest technologies.

I am proficient in a wide range of web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, and MySQL. I also have experience in using popular content management systems such as WordPress, Joomla, and Drupal. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies and attending web development conferences and meetups. I also enjoy travelling and photography, which I often incorporate into my work.
